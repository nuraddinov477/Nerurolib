## 📖 O'zbek Kitoblar Qo'shish:

### **Agar o'zbek kitoblar kerak bo'lsa:**

#### **Usul 1: Google Drive**
1. PDF faylni Google Drive ga yuklang
2. "Ulashish" → "Havola olish"
3. Havolani nusxalang
4. AdminAutoImport.js da havolani almashtiring

#### **Usul 2: Dropbox**
1. PDF faylni Dropbox ga yuklang
2. "Ulashish" → "Havola yaratish"
3. Havolani nusxalang
4. Kodda almashtiring

#### **Usul 3: O'z Serveringiz**
1. PDF faylni serverga yuklang
2. To'g'ri URL ni oling
3. Kodda almashtiring
